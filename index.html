About

Code is Poetry

My name is Collin Green

I like making things. I get equally excited by creating a site to manage our pingpong ladder at work as I do by making a new web app that matches the future doctors of the world with the perfect med schools.

I believe in the open source movement and the idea that innovation requires both community and competition. Below you'll find some of my projects, both public and private. Many of them are free and open source - please take them, learn from them, expand on them, and make the world better.

 

We live in very exciting times -- software has become almost infinitely scalable with minimal effort, and the great minds of the industry have been completely unleashed, empowered by nearly unlimited venture capital to pursue whatever problems they are inspired to address.

Every few years, someone creates something new and completely changes the world, and nothing could be cooler than that.



Talk is cheap. Show me the code.

- Linus Torvalds
